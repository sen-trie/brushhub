About BrushHub

What is BrushHub?

BrushHub is a frontend showcase aimed at demonstrating the concept and user experience of an artwork commissioning platform. This project primarily focuses on building the design and UX of an online e-commerce platform. Thus, it does not include any backend services or database management systems - any changes you made will not be saved.
